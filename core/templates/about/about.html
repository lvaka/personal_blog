About Nerd Hax

Your mission, if you choose you accept it, is to be your awesome self and engange with other humans in meaningful ways.

I started this site because I started seeing so many of my nerd heroes and nerd contemporaries acting as fools and getting themselves canceled.

I myself am a socially awkward individual that has more fun staying inside than going out and sociallizing. Not too long ago, I was a socially awkward individual that was afraid of rejection and desperate to gain the affections of the opposite sex.

Through my journey, I have found a few hacks along the way that have helped me find a woman that I love for the weirdo she is that loves me back for the crazy I am.

This site is a resource, my legacy I leave for all those nerds out there that have no idea how to interact with other people or the opposite sex for that matter. Disclaimer: Not all my hacks may work for you. You are a unique individual, so part of your journey is really up for you to travel and discover. How exciting!
